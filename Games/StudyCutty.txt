==Fruit Slicing Game==

Begin with start button.

Have a "How to Play" option
   --If they click the "yes" button, take to how to play page then the
     next screen.
   --If they click the next button instead, menu fades items to slice are thrown up.

The game begins...

The game throws more items to slice.
   --There is more to slice the longer you play.
The player has two options.

   --Option 1: Hit.. user slices the item and gets points.
   --Option 2: Miss.. user loses and is shown their score.

Game is over after loss.
  -- Option to play again.
  -- Option to use score to buy new "slices" or "items"

--------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes:
*Items are etsu related or school  related.
*High score is based on the amount of points.
*There will always be a "How to Play" button in the corner incase
 user forgets the instructions on how to play.
